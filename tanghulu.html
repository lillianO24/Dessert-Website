Origins

Tanghulu has been a popular dessert for around 800 years in China, and it appeared around the Song Dynasty. This particular period was known for innovations in the culinary area, resulting in this amazing snack. Tanghulu is also very significant in cultural heritage. It was often used during culinary practices and was also used for medicinal purposes as well. One of the more popular ingredients, hawthorn berries, are known for health benefits and are used in Chinese medicine. However, overtime, more fruits were popularized for tanghulu as well.
